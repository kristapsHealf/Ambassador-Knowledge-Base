Frequency Indicators

Priority Levels

P1 - Urgent
Critical business impact. Customer completely blocked or revenue at risk. Response required same day.
P2 - High
Significant impact. Customer frustrated but has workaround available. Response within 24 hours.
P3 - Normal
Minor friction. No blocker, general questions or guidance. Response within 48 hours.
P4 - Low
Nice-to-have. Educational questions or feature requests. Response as time permits.
